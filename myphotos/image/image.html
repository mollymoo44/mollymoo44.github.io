Westward Ho! is a small village in the North of Devon.
It is a popular holiday destination with people from around the U.K for its beautiful
beach, shops and stylish accommodation. For such a small village Westward Ho! packs an
impressive amount of highly rated accommodation, featuring various apartments, caravans
and even bungalows; not to mention the range of rentable local housing.
